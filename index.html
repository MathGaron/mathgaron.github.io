AI/Computer Vision Researcher

Hey there! I am a Computer Vision and AI researcher holding a PhD based near Montreal, Canada. With a track record of nine years in active research and publishing, I am dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology and making an impact in the field of image creation.


Background

I completed my PhD at Université Laval in the Electrical and Computer Engineering department under the guidance of Professor Jean-François Lalonde and Professor Denis Laurendau in 2021. Throughout my academic journey, I gained valuable experience through internships at renowned institutions such as Adobe Research and Facebook Reality Labs (now Meta.). In 2021, I embarked on a new venture by co-founding Depix , a pioneering startup in the field of generative AI and computer vision, where I have played an integral role in its success ever since.


Impact

I've had the privilege of publishing to various international journals and conferences, including CVPR, ECCV, TVCG, and ISMAR, where my research primarily focused on 3D object tracking and integrating virtual objects into photographs for Augmented Reality applications. I've been fortunate to collaborate with exceptional researchers and engineers in the industry, contributing to various patent filings, with Adobe and Depix. Additionally, I've played a crucial role in assembling and leading large teams with diverse skill sets, fostering collaboration and innovation. From founding Robocup Ulaval to my role in co-founding Depix, I've cultivated a passion for developing multidisciplinary expertise and continually enhancing my leadership skills.
